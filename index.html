Dive Into HTML 5

Dive Into HTML 5 covers HTML 5 and other emerging web standards. I am publishing drafts online as I go. Please send feedback. The final version will be published on paper by O’Reilly, under the Google Press imprint. (The printed book’s name is still TBD.) The book will remain online under the CC-BY-3.0 license.

About The Book

Dive Into HTML 5 comprises original prose and graphics licensed under the CC-BY-3.0 license. It is typeset in Essays and Linux Libertine, with public domain illustrations from the Open Clip Art Library and other sources.

The online edition uses jQuery, released under the MIT and GPL licenses. Color syntax highlighting is provided by prettify.js, other parsing code is adapted from highlighter.js; both are released under the Apache License 2.0.
